I would describe my style of cooking as varied and although I do have a particular love of Asian cuisine and often fuse different Asian flavours together (Indian, Thai, Malaysian, Indonesian, Chinese) , I love cooking (and eating) food from around the world, from classic and simple or more elaborate English dishes to French, Italian, Spanish, Greek, Middle Eastern, Afro Caribbean and more.
I love to try out new dishes and enjoy sometimes taking a classic recipe and putting my own twist on it but whatever I cook,it is always prepared with care and I consider my paying clients to be my personal guests so I want them to feel that and I hope that it's reflected in my food.
I feel very happy to think that people will hopefully leave not only with memories of the beautiful surroundings and fun had in Chamonix but also remembering the delicious food that they were served too.

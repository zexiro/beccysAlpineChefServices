Firstly, thanks for visiting my website and I hope you like what you see.
Just as an introduction, I am originally from Windsor in the UK and since 2007 I have been lucky enough to live in the beautiful French alpine town of Chamonix where from 2007 until 2019 I commuted from , flying to the UK to work as cabin crew for a major airline , travelling the world and discovering, sampling, then cooking amazing cuisines .
My love and passion for cooking undoubtedly came from my Mum Claire, who was a wonderful cook and encouraged us as children to try ourselves (making lots of mess in the kitchen in the process! ). As a result, my sister, brother and myself all have a very healthy and appreciative relationship with food.
For a long time I had wanted to venture into catering in some form but commuting and a full time flying career meant that was not possible. Then, after I stopped flying in 2019 and after taking time out I decided to pursue some of my ideas but the Covid pandemic put a halt to progress until during the French lockdown I decided to start a small takeaway food business venture from my apartment when most, or many restaurants were closed , cooking mainly Asian cuisine and this went so well that I actually had to turn down orders some weeks.
After the lockdowns had been lifted in the winter of 2021 I was invited by a chef friend to take on some private chef work in a luxury chalet in the Chamonix Valley during the ski season and after meeting the chalet company owner I accepted bookings with them, cooking for 10-12 clients for a week each time, designing, shopping for, prepping , cooking and serving 4 course meals each night with hosts to help and preparing a continental and hot breakfast each morning.
After this, more bookings came my way and although it was certainly a challenge, I knew that it was one that I was up to so since then and for both the 2021/22 and 2022/23 winter seasons I have continued to build on the successful start I made and to date have been able to deliver a bespoke private chef service to many clients and I have been really happy with the feedback I've received so I'm excited for the upcoming winter season and beyond!

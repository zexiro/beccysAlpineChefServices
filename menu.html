Other options are available

The examples I have shown will hopefully give you a good idea of the food I can offer and as already mentioned, there are other dishes available , including a cheeseboard either in place of or in addition to dessert ,depending on budget. When you enquire about a booking I will send you a questionnaire to answer with allergies, dietary requirements and other information so that I can propose a menu based on your preferences but please note, it is not possible to book several different choices of dishes for each group so the finalised menu will be one that the whole group will eat, with the exception of any allergies or specific dietary needs as mentioned before and that includes a separate child's meal sitting which can be accommodated.
